Let us start

Studying in Graz often means that you have to move out from home and start living on your own. This can be a challenge. Especially width limited money and resources.

Hence, I have created this short Documentation for you.

Accommodation

First and foremost you need a place to sleep. Luckily there are several different options.

Students home

This is the option, if you need some help cleaning, because in most accommodations like this, there is cleaning staff. This is also a great option, if you like getting to know new people and don't need too much space.

WGs

Here, you share your apartment with 2, 3 or sometimes 4 people. If you have friends, who want to live in Graz too, this is a great option.

Keep in mind that here no cleaning is done for you like in a Students Home

Food

In my opinion this is the most important area of this page.

Eating enough food while ensuring you are not overspending can be a huge problem for students. There are several ways to deal with this problem.

For example, I have a job additional to my studies in order to be able to afford enough good food. I am a very hungry person, so without a job, it would be very difficult to get my calories in

Another option is eating solely at places with student discounts.

Conclusion

  • Live in a Students Home
  • or in a shared apartment
  • work somewhere
  • or look for food discounts
  • enjoy your time
